About Our Mission

Empowering Local Businesses

4S Collective is a student-led consulting initiative through Del Norte High School's DECA program. We specialize in helping local restaurants and small businesses optimize their operations, enhance their digital presence, and connect with their communities.

Our team combines fresh perspectives with proven business strategies, offering affordable solutions that drive real results. We understand the unique challenges facing local businesses and provide personalized strategies that fit your budget and goals.
